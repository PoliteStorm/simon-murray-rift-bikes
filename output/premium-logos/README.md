# RIFT Premium Logo Variations

This directory contains **14 sophisticated logo variations** that reflect a premium custom bike company at the edge of excellence - Tour de France level sophistication without being tacky.

## Design Philosophy

These logos embody:
- **Premium Craftsmanship**: Sophisticated, refined designs
- **Edge of Excellence**: Breaking through boundaries
- **Tour de France Level**: World-class performance aesthetic
- **Abstract Sophistication**: Not literal, not tacky - pure elegance
- **Rift Concept**: Breaking through, reaching the edge, pushing limits

## Logo Variations

### 1. Geometric Rift
**Abstract geometric representation of a rift** - Two planes separating with energy lines in the gap. Clean, modern, sophisticated.

### 2. Velocity Streak
**Speed and velocity lines representing motion** - Flowing curves that suggest speed and forward momentum. Dynamic yet refined.

### 3. Horizon Break
**Horizon breaking apart - edge of universe** - A horizon line with breaking points and ascending energy. Represents breaking through limits.

### 4. Diamond Split
**Diamond shape splitting - premium geometric** - A diamond cleaving apart with energy between. Geometric precision meets dynamic movement.

### 5. Radial Breakthrough
**Radial energy breaking through** - Energy rays emanating from a central point, breaking through barriers. Power and precision.

### 6. Angular Momentum
**Angular momentum - dynamic sophistication** - Rotating angular lines suggesting momentum and forward motion. Technical yet elegant.

### 7. Precision Edge
**Precision edge - clean and technical** - A sharp edge line with precision marks, breaking through. Technical excellence.

### 8. Light Beam
**Light beam cutting through space** - A glowing beam of light with particles. Represents cutting through to the edge.

### 9. Geometric Flow
**Geometric flow pattern** - Flowing geometric shapes that suggest movement and energy. Abstract and sophisticated.

### 10. Minimal Break
**Minimal break - ultra clean** - A single clean line with a subtle break point. Ultra-minimalist sophistication.

### 11. Energy Cascade
**Energy cascade - dynamic premium** - Cascading energy lines flowing downward. Dynamic and premium.

### 12. Abstract Peak
**Abstract peak - reaching the edge** - An abstract peak formation breaking through upward. Reaching new heights.

### 13. Serif Elegance
**Elegant serif with subtle icon** - Playfair Display serif typography with a subtle abstract element. Classic luxury.

### 14. Tech Precision
**Tech precision - futuristic clean** - Precision grid lines with a break point. Futuristic and technical.

## Design Principles

All logos follow these principles:

1. **Not Tacky**: No obvious bike wheels, chains, or literal cycling elements
2. **Abstract Sophistication**: Geometric, abstract forms that suggest concepts
3. **Premium Aesthetic**: Clean lines, refined typography, sophisticated color use
4. **Edge Concept**: Breaking through, reaching limits, pushing boundaries
5. **Tour de France Level**: World-class, elite, precision-focused

## Typography

Logos use premium fonts:
- **Inter**: Modern, clean, professional
- **Montserrat**: Elegant, geometric
- **Space Grotesk**: Tech-forward, unique
- **Orbitron**: Futuristic, precise
- **Playfair Display**: Classic luxury serif

## Viewing the Variations

### Option 1: Preview HTML (Recommended)
Start a web server and open the preview:

```bash
cd output/premium-logos
python3 -m http.server 8004
```

Then open: **http://localhost:8004/preview.html**

### Option 2: Direct SVG Files
Double-click any `.svg` file to view it directly in your browser.
