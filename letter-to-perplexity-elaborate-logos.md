- Multiple layers of visual elements

- **Textured Typography**: Letters with patterns, gradients, or material textures
- **Ornamental Typography**: Decorative elements integrated into letterforms
- **Kinetic Typography**: Dynamic, motion-suggesting letterforms
- **Layered Typography**: Multiple text layers with different opacities and effects
- **Metallic/Chrome Effects**: Gold and emerald with realistic material properties
- **Glow and Halo Effects**: Luminous typography with light emissions
- **Embossed/Debossed**: Text that appears raised or recessed

### 3. **Elaborate Icon Integration**
- Icon and text fused together (not just side-by-side)
- Icon elements breaking through or wrapping around text
- Icon as part of the letterforms themselves
- Multiple icon variations (different scales, rotations, positions)
- Icon with decorative frames, borders, or ornamental elements

### 4. **Advanced Visual Effects**
- **Gradients**: Multi-stop gradients in emerald and gold
- **Shadows**: Multiple shadow layers for depth
- **Glows**: Inner and outer glows in brand colors
- **Patterns**: Geometric patterns, textures, or decorative motifs
- **Filters**: SVG filters for blur, lighting, and effects
- **Masks**: Complex masking for sophisticated reveals
- **Blending Modes**: Different blend modes for visual richness

### 5. **Sophisticated Layouts**
- **Asymmetric Compositions**: Dynamic, off-center layouts
- **Overlapping Elements**: Text and icon overlapping in interesting ways
- **Negative Space**: Clever use of negative space for visual interest
- **Framing Elements**: Decorative borders, frames, or containers
- **Ornamental Accents**: Flourishes, decorative lines, geometric patterns

### 6. **Material & Texture Expression**
- **Metallic Surfaces**: Realistic gold and emerald metal textures
- **Carbon Fiber**: Subtle carbon fiber patterns (bike-related)
- **Brushed Metal**: Directional texture effects
- **Glossy/Matte Contrasts**: Mix of surface finishes
- **Depth Through Lighting**: Realistic lighting and shading
